# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in the WorkClaw community a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Provide actionable technical feedback.
- Respect boundaries, privacy, and different viewpoints.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional open-source community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

For severe or repeated violations, maintainers may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

## Reporting

To report behavior concerns, contact maintainers via project support channels in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).
